beanmachine.ppl.experimental.neutra.iafflow

Module contents

Implements inverse autoregressive flows.

reference:

Germain, Mathieu, et al. “Made: Masked autoencoder for distribution estimation.” International Conference on Machine Learning. 2015. http://proceedings.mlr.press/v37/germain15.pdf (MADE)

Improved Variational Inference with Inverse Autoregressive Flow, Kingma et al June 2016 https://arxiv.org/abs/1606.04934 (IAF)

class beanmachine.ppl.experimental.neutra.iafflow.InverseAutoregressiveFlow(based_distribution, network_architecture, length: int, dim: int, node: beanmachine.ppl.model.rv_identifier.RVIdentifier, world: beanmachine.ppl.world.world.World, stable: bool)

Bases: torch.nn.modules.module.Module

A IAF Model is a (prior, flow) pair. We try to calculate the evidence lower bound (ELBO) is calculated in z space instead of original θ space,using by default Eq (3) from Hoffman, Matthew, et al. (2019).

L(φ) = A − KL(q(θ) || p(θ)) = integrals q(z) log π(f(z))/(q(z)|∂f/∂z |^−1) dz Eq (3)

Thus we compute Monte Carlo estimate of the ELBO by evaluating the log-ratio in Eq (3) at a z sampled from q(z). Use estimates to maximize the ELBO in z space, and therefore minimize the KL divergence from q(θ) to p(θ).
